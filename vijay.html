Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor, singer, dancer, and philanthropist. He's one of the highest-paid actors in India and is known for his work in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974 in Madras to director S. A. Chandrasekhar. Vijay made his acting debut as a child in the 1984 film Vetri, directed by his father. He played his first lead role in Naalaiya Theerpu in 1992. He's won multiple awards, including eight Vijay Awards and three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. In February 2024, he announced his retirement from acting and his entry into politics.
